BountyHunter$: Patriots always answer the call

Details about this project:

This game represents a demo of a concept. The idea of the game is you play as an assassin that has to kill different targets to progress the story. For a more indeph description of the concept and story you can read the GDD provided in the link bellow.

The playable demo strips the idea to its most fundamental concepts. You have to hunt the npc displayed on the device on your left. You have access to four weapons (a crossbow (scrollwheel down), a sniper(scrollwheel up), a knife(3) and a pistol (4)). To kill the target you have find it and shoot it. The npc can alert other NPCs that will start to attack you
